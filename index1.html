About Me

My name is Dr. John Bartholet, and I’m thrilled to help you achieve optimal health and wellness!

Born and raised in Omaha, I graduated from Creighton Prep High School and went on to earn a Bachelor of Science in Biology from Creighton University in 1997. I’ve spent years in the health field, starting at my family’s pharmacy, where I learned the importance of health, nutrition, and holistic wellness.

I received my Doctor of Chiropractic degree from Cleveland Chiropractic College in 2002, where I also served as a teaching assistant. Continuous learning is crucial, so I regularly attend seminars to stay updated on the latest chiropractic care techniques.

My Approach

  • Respect and Honesty: You’ll be treated with the utmost respect, and I promise transparent communication throughout your care.
  • Holistic Techniques: I use a variety of evidence-based techniques tailored to your needs to address the root causes of your discomfort, not just the symptoms.
